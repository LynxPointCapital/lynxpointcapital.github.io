Leadership

Jacob Brown

Co-Founder & Head Trader

With over a decade of experience in the financial markets, Jacob Brown brings deep expertise, sharp instincts, and disciplined execution to LynxPoint Capital. From an early age, Jacob was drawn to the world of trading, not just for the thrill of the markets, but for the intellectual challenge it demands. Over the years, he has developed and refined a unique trading style rooted in data-driven decision-making, technical precision, and unwavering risk control.

As Head Trader, Jacob leads the firm's market operations and strategy development. He plays a pivotal role in mentoring new talent, designing proprietary systems, and staying ahead of market trends across asset classes. His leadership is defined by calm under pressure, strategic thinking, and an ability to turn market uncertainty into opportunity.

Nicholas Rozon

Co-Founder & Risk Manager

With over 15 years of experience in business development and strategic operations, Nicholas Rozon is the driving force behind the structure and growth of LynxPoint Capital. As Co-Founder and Risk Manager, he ensures the firm operates with precision, creating an environment where traders can perform at their highest level while maintaining disciplined risk protocols.

Nicholas brings a strong background in business relations, systems thinking, and organizational leadership. His ability to connect vision with execution makes him instrumental in shaping the firm's direction, refining internal processes, and managing firm-wide risk with consistency and transparency.

Passionate about performance, Nicholas works closely with the trading team to align goals, enforce accountability, and make sure every decision supports long-term profitability and sustainability.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To shape elite, performance-driven traders through world-class education, cutting-edge technology, and disciplined strategy. At LynxPoint Capital, we bridge the gap between retail and institutional trading by equipping individuals with the tools, training, and support needed to compete and thrive in today's financial markets.

Our Vision

To position LynxPoint Capital as the benchmark for modern proprietary trading: where innovation meets precision, and talent meets opportunity. We envision a world where ambitious traders have the same access to infrastructure, insight, and execution power as top-tier institutions, redefining what's possible for the next generation of professionals.

Our Values

Excellence — We pursue the highest standards across trading, education, and execution — because second-best is not an option.

Integrity — We operate with full transparency and uphold the highest ethical standards in every decision we make.

Innovation — We relentlessly refine our strategies and technologies to stay ahead of evolving market dynamics.

Discipline — We believe consistency is built through structure, accountability, and meticulous risk management.

Collaboration — We cultivate a team-first culture where knowledge is shared, performance is celebrated, and collective success is the standard.
