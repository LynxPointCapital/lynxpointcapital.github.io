About Us

Founded in 2025, Lynx Point Capital emerged from the shared vision of two ambitious trading entrepreneurs determined to elevate the world of day trading.

Frustrated by the overwhelming flood of scattered, often misleading information in the industry, we set out to build something different, something more focused and refined.

Lynx Point Capital was born with a dual purpose: to bring together elite traders under one roof and to shape the next generation of high-performance professionals.

At our core, we are a proprietary trading firm committed to precision, discipline, and results.

We don't just trade, we cultivate excellence.
